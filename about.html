About LMHBlockchain

Overview

LMH Blockchain supplements public and private blockchains with adaptive authentication solutions. Its authentication hub enforces the right level of authentication based upon AI-powered rules and risk scoring. Low-risk/low-value activity like checking an account balance may require only simple authentication while high-risk/high-value activity like trade finance transactions may require multi-factor authentication, combined with sophisticated biometrics. Our products include patent-pending biometric user authentication for VPN, corporate approvals and payments, retail user access and payments, and hands-free voice command services. Our solutions enable users to securely access enterprise environments, approve high-value wires and brokerage orders, sign in and make bill payments without PINs and passwords, and manage financial functions with just the sound of their voices. Only Say-Tec's solution combines security with an ultimate user experience using full 3-factor authentication.

Our Mission
